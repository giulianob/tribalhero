Download AIR SDK: http://airdownload.adobe.com/air/win/download/14.0/AIRSDK_Compiler.zip
and extract to c:\airsdk

Download Flash Player Standalone http://download.macromedia.com/pub/flashplayer/updaters/14/flashplayer_14_sa_debug.exe

In FlashDevelop, configure the AIR SDK to point to c:\airsdk
then configure in FlashViewer > External Player Path the path to the standalone flash player
